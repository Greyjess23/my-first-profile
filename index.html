A little bit about me...

All of my adult life I have been a healthcare provider working in hospitals, nursing homes, and even most recently working on the ambulance running 911 calls in my community. With everything 2020 I was ready for a change but I didn't know which way to go until I started looking into technology, honestly, mostly due to the recent influx of companies coming into my state.

I realized that I was already problem solving in healthcare just in different ways than I am currently in the tech field, the stakes are different between both fields but I believe attention to detail and effectiveness are highly important in both.

I started to enjoy solving problems in JavaScript and learning and styling HTML and CSS more than practicing healthcare so I decided to change my career path and focus on software development.

I am looking forward to the possibilities of combining my decade of healthcare knowledge, skills, and experience with my new technological knowledge to advance healthcare or how healthcare is provided one day.

On top of all that I am a wife and mother to three children the youngest which is still in diapers. The idea that I could have a productive career and not have to sacrifice time with my family is my strongest drive.
